Slug Hemligssen





Slug Hemligssen is a spy working for King Gjuki Sorvott IV of Jatizso. He is dressed up as a jester and has been trying to learn more Information from King Mawnis. He broke his arm entertaining him and has yet to get any information from him.

Players can not go through him they have to go around him.
