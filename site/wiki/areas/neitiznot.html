Neitiznot







Neitiznot ( Nay-tizz-not ) is the sister island of Jatizso.



Getting There

  • Head to Rellekka and from there head towards the dodcks and catch the boat to Neitiznot.

Features

  • Mining spots near banks
  • The Geyser is a water source near a bank ( Good to filling your vials )
  • The spinning wheel is also near the bank
  • There is a furnace near the bank
  • There is a range near the bank
  • You can cure yak hides near a bank
  • Tons of Yaks which can be farmed for their hides


History

A Fremennik king banished his two youngest sons, Neitiznot and Jatizso, for their rivarly between each other. Shortly after their banishment, they crossed two island that were near Rellekka. Neitiznot chose the island to the west and name it after himself. His brother Jatizso demanded the island for his own. Neitiznot told him to screw off and the fighting continued. To this day, the people of Neitiznot still have a fierce with citzens of Jatizso.

Climate and Geography

Neitiznot is in the North which means it snows much of the year and the cold winter winds go through it year round. Neitiznot has amazing soil for growing vegetation. It is divided into two islands The southern and northern island. The southern part is where the settlements are with the yaks. The northern is infested with trolls who are fighting the honor guards. It does have arctic pine trees and linked to a small island to the northeast.
