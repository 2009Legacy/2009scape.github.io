Agility Guide

Introduction | Agility Courses | Minigames | Boosts




Introduction


Agility is the dreaded skill for some and the relaxing skill for others. Unlike Osrs, You will be taken back to the era where roof top hopping was never a thought and the only way to train this skill was to power through it or play minigames.

In this guide we will be using both player made guides and just pure knowledge of how the game worked and what is inside of 2009scape. This page is a work in progress like the game itself. More content will be added to the game so more will be added to this Wiki in time.

Minigames

Agility Pyramid - A dangerous Agility training area located between Sophanem and Nardah. Players have to climb up to the top of pyramid and get the pyramid top and then make it back down to the bottom to hand it to Simon Templeton.

Requirements
  • Level 30 Agility
  • At least 5 waterskins.
  • Recommended to bring food incase you fail.


Brimhaven Agility - The adventure of a life time Capn Izzy promises you if you go down and discover more of the dungeons we had found.


Requirements
  • 200 gp
  • Recommended to bring food incase you fail.


Methods of Restoring Energy


Item Name


Item Image


Effect

Energy Potion Restores 20% of run energy per dose, up to four doses per potion
Super energy potion Restores 40% of run energy per dose, up to four doses per potion
Spirit terrorbird, Tireless Run special move Restores a portion of your run energy based on your Agility level
Explorer's Ring Restores 50% of your run energy a number of times per day (depending on the level of the ring)
