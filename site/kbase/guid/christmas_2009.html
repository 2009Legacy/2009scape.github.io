Christmas 2009 Event Guide

Introduction | The Event | Rewards


Introduction


The Land of Snow is once again open to visitors from the realm of RuneScape, for the Queen of Snow is throwing a lavish Christmas feast for all and sundry. Or, at least, she was, but some miserly old sort has stolen all the posh nosh! Oh, and locked Santa in a cage for good measure.

The Event

If you fancy a feast this Christmas - indeed, if you fancy a Christmas this Christmas - you're going to have to head to the Land of Snow and buddy up with a snow imp to take on the food thief, Ebenezer Scourge. The Land of Snow can be reached through the Christmas cupboards (now with realistic snow effectï¿½) found north of Falador, behind the Wise Old Man's house in Draynor Village, and in Varrock's market square.

Once there, simply talk to a snow imp to get cracking - who knows, it could even be your snow imp pal from last year's frost-filled afair. Whichever imp ends up at your side, they'll probably have a plan to scare up some grub.







Rewards

Once you've stitched together a plan of action, rattled some chains and spooked a scary old man into mending his ways, you should have a chat with the newly-freed Santa before claiming your reward from under the Christmas tree in the Land of Snow. You'll also be free to help yourself to the now fully-laden tables of food (the food can only exist in the Land of Snow, though).
