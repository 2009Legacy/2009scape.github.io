Agility - Ape Atoll

Introduction | Location | The Course | Rewards


Introduction

An overview of the Ape Atoll Agility Course
The Ape Atoll Agility Course is a high level obstacle course located south of Marim on Ape Atoll. You will need to have finished Monkey Madness to unlock the Ape Atoll Agility Course.

To use the course you will also need to carry a monkey greegree made from a large or small ninja monkey. If you have lost your greegree you will need to make a new one.

To make a new greegree you can purchase a monkey talisman from Tutab's Magical Market, located just north of the agility course. You will then have to repeat the method used during the quest in order to turn it into a greegree A monkey talisman will cost 1000 coins. To talk to Tutab you will, of course, need to be wearing a monkeyspeak amulet. To make a new amulet you will have to repeat the method you used during the Monkey Madness quest.

Location

Location of the Agility Course
Ape Atoll can be reached by glider or by using the Teleport to Ape Atoll spell. To travel by glider you will need to speak to Daero in Blurberry's Bar in the Gnome Stronghold. You will then fly to Crash Island and from there catch a boat to Ape Atoll.
