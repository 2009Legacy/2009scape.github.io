Dungeoneering - Mining Tables

Introduction | Pickaxes | Mining Ore


Introduction

To use a pickaxe in Daemonheim, you must be raiding a dungeon on the following complexity level:

Click here to read more about Mining in RuneScape.

Complexity level 2+ - Mining


Pickaxes

As with above-ground RuneScape, you may use a pickaxe if you have the Mining level to do so. Pickaxes may be purchased from the smuggler, smithed at an anvil, or won in battle.

(m) = Members Only

Mining Ore

Seams of strange ore can be found in the floors of Daemonheim, and they can be mined by anyone with a pickaxe and the Mining level required to do so. Ore can then be taken to a furnace, often found in the starting room of a floor, to be smelt into bars, ready to be smithed into weapons and armour.

(m) = Members Only
