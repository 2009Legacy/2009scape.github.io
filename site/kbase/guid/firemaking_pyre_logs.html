Firemaking - Pyre Logs

Introduction | The Pyre Logs


Introduction

Sacred oil is made during the Shades of Mort'ton quest. To obtain this oil you will need to rebuild and restore the temple in Mort'ton. When the temple is built, light the fire in the center of the temple and use olive oil on the flame. You will now make some sacred oil. The olive oil can be obtained from the shop owner, Razmire Keelgan.

To make pyre logs, you will need any type of log and some sacred oil. To obtain different types of logs see the Woodcutting Guide.


After acquiring the sacred oil, use the vial on any logs and you will turn them into pyre logs. You will then be able to light the pyre logs on the funeral pyres around Mort'ton - providing you have shade remains, which are the remains from defeated shades.

Burning pyre logs in Mort'ton will help you obtain the key to a chest that contains fine cloth, used to create splitbark armour for mages.

If you have completed Legacy of Seergaze, you will also be able to burn Vyrewatch remains in the Paterdomus Columbarium using pyre logs. Vyrewatch corpses can only be burnt using teak pyre logs or better.
