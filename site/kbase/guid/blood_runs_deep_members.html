Blood Runs Deep (Members)

Introduction | Information | Spoilers | Developers
Introduction
A stranger has washed up on the shores of Lunar Isle, broken and delirious, and Baba Yaga requests your aid in getting him back on his feet. Yet beware the horrors that lurk in his mind, for they could prove to be a very real threat for Fremennik everywhere...
Quest Information
Members only: Yes
Start point: Baba Yaga's house on Lunar Isle
To Start: Speak to Baba Yaga
Quest Length: Long
Minimum Requirements:
Dream Mentor
Glorious Memories
Horror from the Deep
Level 65 Slayer
Level 75 Attack
Level 75 Strength
Completed all easy, medium and hard tasks in the Fremennik Achievement Diary
High Magic and Ranged is also recommended
