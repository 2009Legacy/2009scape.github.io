Crafting - Spinning

Introduction | Spinning Wheel Locations


Introduction

To spin the items show below, simply locate a spinning wheel. You can locate spinning wheels by finding this icon on your minimap . If you have the correct items in your inventory, select what you want to make from the list below to craft that item and gain Crafting experience.

Spinning Wheel Locations

When you have located a spinning wheel, click on the 'Spin' option to bring up the spinning options. From this menu you can select the item you wish to create, if you have the necessary Crafting level and materials required. You can also right-click on your desired item in the interface to bring up a submenu. On this menu you will find the make 1, 5, All and X options.
