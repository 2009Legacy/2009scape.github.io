Construction - Quest Hall

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

The quest hall is a nice area to separate your house a bit, while also giving you the chance to show off some of your quest achievements.

Key Features

A typical quest hall
The main focal point in this room is the stairs. This is another room where you are able to construct stairs which give the option to go up or down a floor. (Your house can be built up to two floors high, as well as one floor below ground.)

Also in this room, you can set up some of your quest items on display to show off your quest achievements. A useful point to this is that the amulet of glory, when mounted on the wall, can be used infinitely to teleport. When you remove the mounted items, you get the original item back, only your building supplies are used.

If you have visited the Herald and have completed enough relevant quests to buy a portrait or landscape picture from him, you can display it in this room. When you remove a portrait, landscape or map, you do not get it back

Bookcases in any room contain useful quest books from quests you have completed: simply browse the bookcase for a book, remove it and read it if you wish to remember parts, plots and histories of some quests.

The Hotspots

Quest hall hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are eight types of hotspot in the quest hall that you can construct on:

  • Stair/Rug - (you do not have to build stairs)
  • Guild trophy
  • Portrait
  • Landscape
  • Sword
  • Map
  • Bookcase
  • Room
