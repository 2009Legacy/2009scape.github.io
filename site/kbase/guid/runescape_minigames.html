Activities

Barbarian Assault
Can you survive the waves of the Penance and prove your worth to the barbarians?
Brimhaven Agility Arena
Fun and adventure awaits you in this ancient arena.
Castle Wars
Will your team be victorious in this combat-based team game?
Clan Wars
Fight with up to one hundred clan-mates.
The Duel Arena
Fight in duels and duel tournaments.
The Fishing Trawler
Can you and your friends help Murphy? If you can he will give you a share of the catch.
Fist of Guthix
It's hunter vs. hunted in this fast-paced contest to gather power.
Games Room
Visit the Prince of Burthorpes games room and challenge a fellow player to a friendly game.
The Great Orb Project
Gather arcane power and prove thaumaturgic theorems.
Mage Training Arena
Gain magic experience through four tasks as well as earn points to trade for items in the Magic Training Arena shop.
Mobilising Armies
Lead your army to fame, glory and treasure.
Pest Control
Can you protect the Void Knight whilst fighting back hordes of creatures?
Shades of Mort'ton
Earn Prayer and Firemaking experience whilst cremating the shades of Mort'ton.
Soul Wars
Creation vs. Destruction.
Stealing Creation
Steal the stuff of the universe and defeat your foes!
Temple Trekking / Burgh de Rott Ramble
Can you defend a villager through the perils of Morytania?
Trouble Brewing
Race to brew rum in this frantic piratical contest!
TzHaar Fight Cave
Do you have what it takes to last to the very end?
TzHaar Fight Pit
Are you tough enough to be the last man standing in this fast paced multi-player game?
Vinesweeper
Help Farmer Blinkin find his seeds in this Farming activity, but watch out for those wascaly wabbits.
