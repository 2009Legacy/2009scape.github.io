Construction - Garden

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

This is one of the two level 1 rooms/areas you can make. You will have a garden already when you buy your first house, as you need to have at least one exit portal.

Key Features

An example garden
As touched on above, a garden is a must have as it includes the exit portal. This is where you lock the portal if you wish to lock your house while you are in it; for more information please see the bottom of the basics page.

If you have two gardens (or a formal garden) you do not have to have two portals. Instead, you can build a dungeon entrance, if you have a dungeon below.

Aside from the portal the garden is largely for decoration. Plants can be purchased from the Gardener for a price and planted in your garden for experience.

The Hotspots

Garden hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are eight types of hotspot in the garden that you can construct on:

  • Centrepiece
  • Big tree
  • Tree
  • Small plant 1
  • Small plant 2
  • Big plant 1
  • Big plant 2
  • Room
