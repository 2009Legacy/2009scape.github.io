Introduction
To bury bones in Daemonheim, you must be raiding a dungeon on the following complexity level:

Complexity level 1+ - Combat

Click here to read more about Prayer in RuneScape.


Burying Bones


There are a small number of bones that can be buried to gain experience in Daemonheim. They are as follows:

Bone Type

 Experience Gained

Bones		 4.5

Bat bones		 5.3

Big bones		 15

Dragon bones		 72
