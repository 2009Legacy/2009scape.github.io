Agility - Wilderness Course

Introduction | Location | The Course | Rewards


Introduction

The dangerous rope swing
The Wilderness is a relic of the god wars, long scarring the surface of RuneScape. In the last days of those frightful times, the world was in ruins and the dead lay unburied across many fields once green and pleasant - and even in those awful days, the area we now call the Wilderness was the most defiled.

In modern days, the land has still not recovered and has become a haven for criminals, violent monsters...and those adventurers willing to risk their lives for the Agility Rewards the very deepest Wilderness offers.

Location

Location of the Agility Course
The Wilderness Agility Course is a high level obstacle course that is found at the very north of the Wilderness, west of the Pirates' Hideout.

As it is situated in such deep Wilderness, some players prefer to use the teleport lever in Ardougne and save a long and hazardous walk.

To enter the course you must cross a long narrow ridge, which requires an Agility level of 52 to attempt (but earning you 15 Agility experience), although you may fail and fall into a pit of white wolves. Throughout this course there is always a risk of failing obstacles and being prey to revenants (who can freeze and teleblock you), so don't forget to bring some food.
