Construction - Dining Room

Introduction | Key Features | The Hotspots | The Items

Introduction

With level 10 comes the ability to build a dining room. Perfect for holding a dinner party and enough room to seat eight visitors, this is the ideal room to have your servant wait on you and your friends!

Key Features

A typical dining room
The main feature of the dining room is the large central table.

If you have built a bell pull, you can summon your servant (if you have one). When they arrive, you can have them prepare food for you and your visitors. He/she will then set the table with the food they have prepared so you can all eat!

Similar to the parlour, there is also a fireplace hotspot, which will give slightly increased experience for lighting, but the fire lasts longer than normal.

This is the first room you will see 'Decoration' hotspots. You can construct items here to show off your family crest.

The Hotspots

Dining room hotspots
As shown in the picture to the left, there are eight types of hotspot in the dining room that you can construct on:
  • Table
  • Fireplace
  • Seating
  • Decoration
  • Bell pull
  • Curtain
  • Room
